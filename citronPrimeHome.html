OUR STORY

At Citron Prime, it is our mission to make gourmet, real, good food accessible. With professionally trained chefs and a fresh, seaside patio, our guests are guaranteed a meal to remember, whether it is a birthday celebration, family get-together, girls' night out, or just a special Sunday! Our founder, Simone Beshtoev, deeply appreciates the power of a good meal in bringing people together. Let Citron Prime be your go-to get-togther location by booking a reservation today.

OUR FOUNDER

Simone Beshtoev, the founder of Citron Prime, was classically trained in gourmet cuisine by the age of 14. Raised in Toronto, she quickly realized she wanted to create something, so moved to France to get training from the top culinary school in Paris. She then returned to North America, where she found a lack of accessible but high-quality restaurants, thus began her new franchise. Simone knows the power a good meal can have in bringing people together, and also has a strong appreciation for vacation. After all, what is better than a beach and being surrounded with loved ones? A seaside patio restaurant with a delicious meal.
